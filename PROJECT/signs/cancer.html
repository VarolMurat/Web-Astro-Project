Personality Section

Deeply intuitive, sentimental, and dreamy, Cancer can be one of the most challenging Zodiac signs to get to know. Emotion runs strong for them, nothing is more important than the family and the home. Cancerians are very Sympathetic and empathetic, Cancerians are greatly attuned to those around them. Cancerians are extremely devoted especially if they truly care about you, making them wonderfully sensitive people to be around. Sun in Cancerians are very loyal and able to empathize with other people's pain and suffering. The moon in astrology is the ruler of emotions, this is why the phases of the lunar cycle affects cancerians and in turn this results in a slew of unpredictable emotional patterns that are beyond their control.
